# Multimedia Trade Secrets & Smartcards

A comprehensive study of hardware security, piracy, and legal protections.

## Project Structure
- **Presentation (LaTeX/Beamer):** Located in `main.tex`.
- **Data & Analytics (Kaggle):** [Link to Kaggle Notebook](https://www.kaggle.com/code/hunainafridi2/notebook6013a2bb9f)

## Key Learning Objectives
1. Understand the 'Shockley-Queisser' limit in solar nanotech (as a parallel to hardware limits).
2. Analyze how 'Fault Injection' compromises trade secrets in Smartcards.
3. Compare physical security vs. legal trade secret protections.
